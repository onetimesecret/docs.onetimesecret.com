## What is the Audit Log?

The Audit Log is a record of meaningful activity across your team's account —
who did what, and when. It is designed to give administrators visibility into
account and sharing activity for security review and compliance purposes.

Expected to cover events such as:

- Account and membership changes.
- Domain and configuration changes.
- Notable secret and sharing activity (without exposing secret contents).
