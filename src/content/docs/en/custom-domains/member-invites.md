## What are Member Invites?

**Member Invites** let you add other people to your organization so they can
help manage your account, domains, and settings. Creating and managing
organization members is part of the **Identity Plus** plan.

Typical uses include:

- Inviting colleagues to administer your custom domains and configuration.
- Granting access without sharing a single shared login.
- Removing access when someone no longer needs it.

## Plan availability

- **Free** and above can create and manage organizations.
- **Identity Plus** adds the ability to **invite and manage members** within
  your organization (up to your plan's member limit — see
  [Compare Plans](/en/pricing/compare-plans)).
- **Team Plus** adds full **team** management and a shared, organization-wide
  view of activity.
