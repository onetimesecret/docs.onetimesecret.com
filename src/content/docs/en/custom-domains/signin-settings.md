## What are Signin Settings?

Signin Settings control how existing users authenticate when they access your
[custom domain](/en/custom-domains). Where [Signup Settings](/en/custom-domains/signup-settings)
govern account _creation_, Signin Settings govern account _access_ — the login
experience your users see on your branded domain.

Typical uses include:

- Choosing which sign-in methods are available on your domain.
- Requiring [Single Sign-On (SSO)](/en/team/sso) so users authenticate through your identity provider.
- Aligning the sign-in experience with your organization's security requirements.

## Related features

- [Signup Settings](/en/custom-domains/signup-settings) — control who can create an account.
- [Single Sign-On (SSO)](/en/team/sso) — let people sign in with their existing corporate credentials.
- [Privacy Options](/en/custom-domains/privacy-options) — broader controls over visitor behavior on your domain.
