## What is the Email Sender?

A custom **Email Sender** is part of the **Identity Plus** plan. It lets the
notifications Onetime Secret sends on your behalf come from an address on your
own domain — for example `secrets@yourbrand.com` — instead of a generic
address, so recipients see a consistent, trusted sender.

Typical uses include:

- Domain-specific notification emails that match your brand.
- A recognizable "From" address that improves trust and deliverability.

## Plan availability

- **Identity Plus** includes a custom sender address for **domain-specific**
  notifications (the `From` address is tied to your custom domain).
- **Team Plus** extends this with a **flexible From domain**, allowing any
  domain in the sender address — not just your custom domain.

Proper email authentication (SPF/DKIM) for your domain is part of getting this
working reliably; specifics will be documented here as the feature ships.
