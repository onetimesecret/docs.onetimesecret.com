## What are Privacy Options?

Enhanced privacy features are part of the **Identity Plus** plan. **Privacy
Options** are the per-domain controls that let you decide how much your
[custom domain](/en/custom-domains) exposes to visitors and how it behaves when
people interact with it.

Depending on your configuration, these controls can include:

- Whether your [branded homepage](/en/custom-domains/homepage-secrets) is public or disabled.
- Whether visitors may create secrets, or only open links you generate.
- How recipients and senders experience your domain.

The goal is to let your custom domain match your organization's privacy posture,
rather than exposing more than you intend.

## Related features

- [Homepage Secrets](/en/custom-domains/homepage-secrets) — turn public secret creation on or off.
