## Why Choose Onetime Secret?

Onetime Secret provides a simple, secure way to share sensitive information. Our service ensures your data remains private and ephemeral in an increasingly permanent digital world.

- **Customizable expiration** - Set your secret to expire after a specific time, from minutes to days
- **Custom domains** - Use your own domain for a personalized and branded experience
- **Open source** - Our transparent, community-audited codebase ensures trust and reliability
