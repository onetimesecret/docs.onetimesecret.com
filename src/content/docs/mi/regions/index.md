Ka whakarato a Onetime Secret i ngā rohe pūtoi raraunga e whā: European Union (EU), United States (US), Canada (CA), me Aotearoa (NZ). Ka āwhina tēnei aratohu i a koe ki te mārama i te tikanga o te kōwhiringa rohe me te āhua o te kōwhiri i te tika mō ō hiahiatanga.

## He Aha Te Tikanga o te Kōwhiringa Rohe

He nui te kōwhiri i te rohe pūtoi raraunga tika mō ētahi take:

1. **Rangatiratanga Raraunga**: He rerekē ngā ture tiaki raraunga me ngā ture o ngā rohe rerekē.
2. **Takamuatanga**: Ko te kōwhiri i tētahi rohe tata atu ki tō pānga kaiwhakamahi matua ka taea te whakaitihia i te takamuatanga.
3. **Ūnga**: He whakahau motuhake ō ētahi whakahaere mō te wāhi ka taea te penapena i ō rātou raraunga.

## Te Hoahoa Kore-Tohatoha

Ka whakamahi a Onetime Secret i tētahi hoahoa kore-tohatoha, e whakaū ana i te motuhake raraunga katoa i waenganui i ngā rohe:

- **Ngā Pūkete Motuhake**: Ko te waihanga pūkete i tētahi rohe e tino motuhake ana i ngā pūkete i ngā rohe kē, ahakoa ka whakamahi koe i te wāhitau īmēra kotahi.
- **Kāore he Mahi Whakawhiti-Pūtoi**: Kāore e taea e koe te mahi (pēnei i te whakawareware karere huna) i ngā pūtoi raraunga. Ka pupuri ia pūtoi i ōna ake karere huna me ngā raraunga kaiwhakamahi.
- **Te Utu Pūmau mō ngā Kaiwhakamahi Utu**: Mō ngā pūkete utu, ahakoa kāore he raraunga kaiwhakamahi e tohatohahia i waenganui i ngā pūtoi, ka mōhiotia tō tūnga ohaurunga puta noa i ngā rohe mā tā mātou kaiwhakarato utu, Stripe.

## Pēhea te Kōwhiri i Tō Rohe

Whakaarohia ēnei āhuatanga i te wā e kōwhiri ana i tō rohe pūtoi raraunga:

### Mō ngā Kaiwhakamahi Kore-Ingoa

- Ka taea te tuku i ngā tono ki onetimesecret.com ki tētahi pūtoi raraunga hohe.
- Ko te wāhi o tō karere huna e mārama tonu mai i te hononga kua waihangahia (e.g., `us.onetimesecret.com/secret/abcd1234`).
- Ka taea e koe te kōwhiri i tētahi wāhi raraunga motuhake mā te whakatere tōtika ki tētahi rohe (e.g., [ca.onetimesecret.com](https://ca.onetimesecret.com/)).

### Mō ngā Kaiwhakamahi Whakamōhio

- I te wā e waihanga ana i tētahi pūkete hou, me kōwhiri koe i tētahi wāhi pūtoi raraunga.
- Me hoki koe ki te wāhi kotahi ki te takiuru.
- Ka noho ngā pūkete me ngā karere huna kei te ora i tō rātou pūtoi raraunga taketake.

### Mō ngā Kaiwhakamahi Katoa

- Ka haere tonu ngā karere huna kua waihangahia me te kore rohe-iti rohenga (e.g., onetimesecret.com/secret/efgh5678) ki te taunoa ki tō mātou pūtoi raraunga EU.
- Ka taea e ngā kaiwhakamahi katoa, utu me koreutu, te kōwhiri i tō rātou pūtoi raraunga i hiahia ai i te wā e waihanga ana i tētahi pūkete.

### Ngā Whakaarohanga Tāpiri

1. **Mō ngā Tangata Takitahi**:
   - Te hiahia whaiaro
   - Te tata ki tō wāhi mō te urunga tere pea
   - Ngā āwangawanga rangatiratanga raraunga whaiaro

2. **Mō ngā Pakihi**:
   - Ngā whakahau ture me te ture
   - Te wāhi o tō pānga kiritaki matua
   - Ngā hiahiatanga ūnga ahumahi-motuhake

3. **Ngā Whakaarohanga Hangarau**:
   - Ngā whakahau takamuatanga mō tō taupānga
   - Te whakaurunga ki ētahi atu ratonga, pūnaha rānei

## Utu me ngā Mahere

Ko tō mātou here ki te wāhi raraunga e toro atu ana ki tō mātou tauira utu:

- Ko ngā utu e hāngai ana ki te wāhi e utu ana koe, ehara i te wāhi kua waihangahia tō pūkete.
- Ko ngā mahere Identity Plus e whai ana i ngā rohe ritenga mutunga kore puta noa i ngā pūtoi raraunga katoa i raro i tētahi ohaurunga kotahi.

## Te Whakatū i Tō Rohe

I te wā e whakatū ana i tō pūkete Onetime Secret, e whirihora ana rānei i tētahi rohe ritenga, ka wātea ki a koe te kōwhiri i tō rohe i hiahia ai. Anei pēhea:

1. Mō ngā pūkete hou: Kōwhiria tō rohe i hiahia ai i te wā o te tukanga rēhita.
2. Mō ngā pūkete kei te ora: Whakapā ki tō mātou tīma tautoko ki te kōrero mō ngā kōwhiringa hekenga rohe.
3. Mō ngā rohe ritenga: Tautuhia tō rohe kua kōwhiria i te wā e whirihora ana i ō tautuhinga DNS (tirohia tō mātou [Aratohu Whakatū Rohe Ritenga](/mi/custom-domains/setup-guide) mō ngā tohutohu taipitopito).

## Ngā Pātai Auau

**P: Ka taea e au te huri i taku rohe i muri i te whakatū i taku pūkete?**
W: Āe, ka taea e koe te huri i tō rohe mā te waihanga pūkete hou ki te wāhitau īmēra kotahi me te whakatere ki te mata pūkete. Mēnā he ohaurunga hohe kei a koe, ka whakahōu aunoa tō pūkete (me whakahōu pea koe i te whārangi).

Kia mōhio:
- Kāore ngā raraunga kei te ora e whakawhiti i waenganui i ngā rohe
- Ka haere tonu ngā hononga karere huna kua waihangahia e koe tae atu ki te tirohanga, ki te pau rānei
- Mō ngā hononga ki ngā rohe ritenga, me:
  1. Tāpiri anō i te rohe ki tō pūkete rohe hou
  2. Whakahōu i ngā tuhinga DNS hono
  3. Whakamahi i tētahi rohe-iti ahurei i te wā e tāpiri anō ana i te rohe ki te karo i ngā tautohetohe ki ngā hononga kei te ora
  4. I muri mai, ka taea e koe te tāpiri i tō rohe i hiahia ai (mēnā e hiahiatia ana) kia taea e koe te tīmata tuku hononga hou ki tō rohe i hiahia ai

**P: Ka pā taku kōwhiringa rohe ki te haumaru o aku karere huna?**
W: Kāo, ko ngā rohe katoa e whakarato ana i te taumata haumaru teitei kotahi. Ko te kōwhiringa matua e pā ana ki te noho raraunga me te takamuatanga pea.

**P: He rerekē ngā utu i waenganui i ngā rohe?**
W: I tēnei wā, ko tō mātou utu e pūmau ana puta noa i ngā rohe katoa. Tirohia tō mātou [whārangi utu](https://onetimesecret.com/pricing) mō ngā kōrero hou rawa.
