# Whakariterite Waitohu Rohe

## Ngā Whakatakotoranga

- Tētahi rohe ritenga kua manatokohia, kei te mahi hoki i tō pūkete Onetime Secret
- Tētahi kōnae ata waitohu i te hōputu PNG, JPG rānei, iti iho i te 1MB te rahi
- Waehere hex mō ngā tae o tō waitohu (hei tauira `#DC4A22`) mēnā e whakamahi ana i ngā tae ritenga

## I te Pūtake
1. Whakatere ki te Papa Tiaki Rohe
2. Tikiake kōnae ata waitohu
3. Whakatakoto waitohu tae
4. Arotake matakite
5. Puritia ngā whakarerekētanga
6. Manatoko whakatinanatanga reo

Hoki ki te Papa Tiaki Rohe, ka taea e koe te whakahohe, whakakore rānei i tō whārangi kāinga tūmatanui me te whakahaere i ō tautuhinga waitohu i ngā wā katoa.

## Whakahaere Ata Waitohu
Tikiake tō ata waitohu waitohu mā te mata whakahaere. Ka whakaaetia e te pūnaha ngā kōnae PNG, JPG rānei iti iho i te 1MB.
