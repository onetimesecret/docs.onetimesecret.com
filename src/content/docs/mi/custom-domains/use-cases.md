## Ngā Keehi Whakamahi mō ngā Rohe Ritenga

Ka whakarato ngā Hononga Karere Muna me ngā Rohe Ritenga i tētahi taumata teitei o te haumaru mō te tohatoha raraunga matatapu me te pupuri i te ōrite waitohu me te hāngaitanga. Anei ētahi huarahi e whakamahi ai ā mātou kiritaki i tā mātou ratonga.

### 1. Te Whakauru Kiritaki, Te Nama rānei

I te wā e tono ana i ngā kōrero matatapu mai i tētahi kiritaki, pērā i ngā taipitopito pūtea, ngā taipitopito kāri nama rānei, tonoa kia tohatoha rātou mā te waihanga i tētahi hononga karere muna i secrets.example.com. Ka whakaū tēnei ka tohatohahia te kōrero ki te haumaru, ā, ko te kaiwhiwhi anake e uru atu ai.

**Painga**: Mā te whakamahi i tētahi rohe ritenga, ka taea e koe te whakapakari i tō tuakiri waitohu me te hanga pono ki ō kiritaki. Ka mōhio te kiritaki ki te rohe, ā, ka maia ake ki te tohatoha i ngā kōrero matatapu.

### 2. Ngā Taipitopito DevOps

I te wā e whakahaere ana i ngā whakatakotoranga i ngā taiao me ngā rōpū maha, tohatohahia ngā taipitopito urunga taupua mā secure.example.com. Ka taea e ngā rōpū whanaketanga te whakawhiti haumaru i ngā kupuhipa pātengi raraunga, ngā tohu API, me ngā kōnae whirihoranga ka pau aunoa i muri i te whakamahi tuatahi, e aukati ana i te whakaaturia o ēnei taipitopito matatapu i roto i ngā pūtahitanga waehere, ngā hōtaka whakawhitiwhiti kōrero, i ngā kōrero īmēra rānei.

**Painga**: Mā te whakaurunga i tō rohe ritenga ki ngā paerunga CI/CD aunoa, ka pupuri koe i ngā tikanga pai haumaru me te pupuri i te whakahaere taipitopito ka ngāwari ki roto i tō rerenga mahi whanaketanga. Ka taea e ngā kawhanaketanga te whakapono ka puta mai ngā taipitopito mai i tētahi puna whakaae, ā, ko te hurihanga aunoa ka whakaū kāore e taea te whakamahi anō i ngā taipitopito kua whakaaturia.

### 3. Te Whakauru Kaimahi

I te wā e kawe ana i ngā mema rōpū hou, whakamāmā i tā rātou urunga rā tuatahi mā te tuku i ngā taipitopito takiuru taupua mā access.example.com. Ka taea e ngā rōpū HR me IT te whakarite i ngā kete whakatau takitahi e whāki ana i ngā kupuhipa tuatahi, ngā whirihoranga VPN, me ngā hononga tuhinga matatapu ka pau i te wā ka urua, e whakaū ana kāore ngā taipitopito e noho i roto i ngā pouaka īmēra, kāore rānei e tukuna hapa.

**Painga**: Mā te whakamahi i tō rohe waitohu mō te tohatoha taipitopito, ka waihanga koe i tētahi wheako whakauru ngaio me te whakatū i ngā tikanga haumaru hauora. Ka taea e ngā kaimahi hou te uru maia ki ngā rauemi e mōhio ana ka puta mai i tētahi puna kamupene e whakaponohia ana.

### 4. Ngā Whakawhitiwhiti HR Tūmataiti

I te wā e whakahaere ana i ngā kōrero matatapu kaimahi pērā i ngā taipitopito utu, ngā nama tautuhi tāke, ngā waehere rēhita painga rānei, tohatohahia ngā raraunga huna mā hrinfo.example.com. Ka taea e ngā rōpū HR te tuku i ngā hononga wā-matatapu ki ngā kaimahi takitahi e whāki ana i ō rātou kōrero whaiaro hei kupu haumaru, e whakaū ana ka pau ngā taipitopito matatapu i muri i te urunga, ā, ka noho tiakina i raro i ngā ture tūmataiti raraunga.

**Painga**: Mā te whakamahi i te rohe waitohu o tō kamupene mō ngā whakawhitiwhiti HR huna, ka pupuri koe i ngā paerewa ngaio me te tutuki i ngā hiahiatanga ūnga mō te whakahaere kōrero tautuhi whaiaro. Ka taea e ngā kaimahi te uru haumaru ki ō rātou kōrero matatapu mā tētahi ara e whakaponohia ana e whakapakari ana i tō herenga ki te tiaki raraunga.

### 5. Urunga Kaimahi Taupua

I te wā e whakahaere ana i ngā kaimahi haumanu huri, ngā kaimahi noho, ngā kaimahi taupua rānei, whakawhitia ngā taipitopito takiuru tuatahi mā tempaccess.example.com ki te haumaru. Ka taea e ngā rōpū whakahaere te waihanga i ngā hononga wā-matatapu e whāki ana i ngā ingoa kaiwhakamahi pūnaha me ngā kupuhipa taupua, kei hea ka pau te karere haumaru e whāki ana i ēnei taipitopito i muri i te tirohanga tuatahi.

Tuhipoka: I te wā e whakatinana ana i tēnei rongoā, whakaūngia ka pupuri tō papa haumaru katoa i te ūnga ki ngā ture tiaki hauora e tika ana pērā i HIPAA, GDPR rānei. Me whakamahi a OneTimeSecret anake mō te whakawhitinga haumaru o ngā taipitopito urunga tuatahi, ā, me whakahaere ngā roanga urunga pūnaha me ngā whakaaetanga mā ō pūnaha whakahaere urunga o tō whakahaere. Ahakoa ka whakarato te ratonga i te haumaru ake i te īmēra mō te tohatoha kōrero matatapu pērā i te PHI, me whakamahi hei rongoā taupua kaua hei rongoā pumau mō ērā raraunga - whakaarohia te whakatinana i ngā pūnaha karere haumaru HIPAA-ūnga hei whakawhitinga PHI auau.

## Whakarāpopoto

Ko te āhei ki te whakaputa i ngā hononga haumaru, taupua-wā mā tō rohe waitohu ka whakarato i te haumaru tāpiri me te kore whakakōpeka i te kaha. Ka whakaatu ia keehi whakamahi me pēhea ngā Rohe Ritenga te whakanui i te tūnga haumaru me te noho ngaio.
