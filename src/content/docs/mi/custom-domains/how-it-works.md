## Tō Waitohu, Tō Mātou Haumaru

Mā te whakamahi i ngā Rohe Ritenga, ehara koe i te tohatoha karere muna noa; kei te whakapakari koe i tō waitohu, te whakanui i te pono, me te whakaū i te ūnga wāhitanga raraunga i ia taunekeneke. Whakapaingahia ki te mahere Identity i tēnei rā ki te wete i tēnei āhuatanga kaha, ā, kawea tō tohatoha karere muna ki te taumata e whai ake nei.

<img src="/img/docs/custom-domains/branded-homepage-enabled.png" alt="Tautuhinga rohe ritenga" width="600" />


## Me Pēhea te Mahi o ngā Rohe Ritenga

1. Me rēhita koe i tētahi rohe, whakamahi rānei i tētahi e pupurihia ana e koe.
2. Kōwhiria tō rohe pūtoi raraunga e pai ana (EU, US rānei).
3. [Whirihorahia ngā tautuhinga DNS o tō rohe](/mi/custom-domains/setup-guide) kia ārahi ki ngā tūmau Onetime Secret i tō rohe kua kōwhiria.
4. Whakatūhia te rohe ritenga i roto i ō tautuhinga pūkete Onetime Secret.
5. I te wā ka manatokohia, ka whakamahi ō hononga karere muna i tō rohe ritenga.
6. [Whakamāorihia te āhua o tō rohe](/mi/custom-domains/brand-guide) me ngā tohu me ngā tae waitohu mā te atanga whakahaere.


## Te Whakatika i ngā Raru Whakatū

- **Tohatoha DNS**: Ka taea pea te tango i te 48 hāora ki te tohatoha katoa. Kia manawanui, ā, whakamātauhia anō i muri mai mēnā ka rahua te manatoko i te tuatahi.
- **Rekoata DNS Hē**: Tirohia anō ō tautuhinga DNS ki ngā tohutohu kua whakaratohia mō tō rohe kua kōwhiria.
- **Raru Tiwhikete SSL**: Whakapā mai ki tō mātou rōpū tautoko mēnā ka pā koe ki ngā raru e pā ana ki SSL.
- **Manatoko Pupuri Rohe**: Whakaūngia kei a koe te whakahaere katoa o te rohe e ngana ana koe ki te whakatū.
- **Kōwhiringa Rohe**: Mēnā kāore koe e mōhio ki tēhea rohe hei kōwhiri, tirohia tō mātou aratohu [Rohe Pūtoi Raraunga](/mi/regions), whakapā rānei ki tō mātou rōpū tautoko.

Hei ako mō ngā tikanga pai mō te whakamahi i ngā Rohe Ritenga ki te haumaru, tirohia tō mātou aratohu [Ngā Tikanga Haumaru Pai](/mi/security-best-practices).
