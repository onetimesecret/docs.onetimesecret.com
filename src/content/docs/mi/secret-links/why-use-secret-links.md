description: Kitea ngā take whakapāpā ki te whakamahi i ngā Hononga Karere Huna mō te tohatoha haumaru, kotahi-wā o ngā kōrero matatapu.
---

I te ao matihiko o nāianei, ko te tohatoha kōrero matatapu ki te haumaru he mea nui ake i mua. Ko ngā Hononga Karere Huna he rongoā pakari ki tēnei wero, e whakarato ana i tētahi whakakotahitanga ahurei o te haumaru, māmā, me te mārie o te whakaaro. Anei ngā take me whakaaro ai koe ki te whakamahi i ngā Hononga Karere Huna:

## 1. Te Haumaru Whakanui

Kua hoahoatia ngā Hononga Karere Huna ki te haumaru hei mea nui rawa:

- **Urunga kotahi-wā**: Ka taea te tiro i ia hononga kotahi anake, e whakaitihia ana te mōrearea o te urunga kāore i whakaaetia.
- **Whakawareware-ā-ia**: I muri i te tirohanga, ka whakakorehia te hononga me ōna ihirangi mō ake tonu, kāore he tapuwae matihiko e toe ana.
- **Te whakawhiti muhumuhu**: Ka muhumuhu ō raraunga pito-ki-pito, e whakaū ana ka noho tiakina i te wā kawe.

## 2. Te Māmā me te Māmā ki te Whakamahi

- **Kāore e hiahiatia he pūkete**: Mō te whakamahi taketake, kāore koe e hiahia ki te waihanga pūkete, ki te maumahara rānei i ngā kupuhipa tāpiri.
- **Atanga hoa-kaiwhakamahi**: Ko te waihanga me te tohatoha i tētahi Hononga Karere Huna he tukanga tōtika e tango i ētahi pāwhiri anake.
- **Te hāngaitanga papa-katoa**: Ka mahi ngā Hononga Karere Huna i runga i tētahi taputapu me tētahi pūtirotiro tukutuku, e māmā ai te tohatoha i ngā pūnaha rerekē.

## 3. Te Whakahaere i te Kōrero kua Tohatohahia

- **Ngā kōwhiringa paunga**: Whakatakotohia ngā wā paunga ritenga mō ō hononga, e hoatu ana i te whakahaere i te roa o te wā e wātea ana te kōrero.
- **Whakawareware i muri i te pānuitanga**: I te wā ka tiro te kaiwhiwhi i te karere huna, ka ngaro mō ake tonu, e whakaū ana kāore te kōrero e noho.
- **Ngā whakamōhiotanga urunga**: (Mō ngā kaiwhakamahi taumata teitei) Whiwhi whakamōhiotanga i te wā kua tirohia tō karere huna.

## 4. Te Ūnga me ngā Tikanga Pai

- **Te whakaitihia raraunga**: Mā te tohatoha kōrero e whakawareware ana, kei te whai koe i ngā mātāpono whakaitihia raraunga e tūtohu ana e te maha o ngā ture tūmataiti.
- **Ngā ara arotake**: (Mō ngā kaiwhakamahi pakihi) Pupuritia te waihanga karere huna me te urunga, e āwhina ana i ngā whakahau ūnga.

## 5. Te Ngāwari

- **Ngā keehi whakamahi maha**: Mai i te tohatoha kupuhipa ki te tuku kōrero pakihi matatapu, he ngāwari ngā Hononga Karere Huna mō ngā horopaki rerekē.
- **Te whakaurunga API**: Mō ngā kaiwhakawhanake, ko tā mātou API ka taea te whakaurunga rere o ngā Hononga Karere Huna ki roto i ō ake taupānga me ngā rerenga mahi.

## 6. Te Mārie o te Whakaaro

- **Te tapuwae matihiko whakaitihia**: Mā te whakamahi i ngā Hononga Karere Huna, ka whakaitihia e koe te nui o te kōrero matatapu e penapena ana i roto i ngā īmēra, ngā rārangi kōrerorero, i ngā ara whakawhitiwhiti pūmau kē rānei.
- **Te maia i te tohatoha**: Tohatohahia ngā kōrero matatapu ki te whakaū kāore e taka ki ngā ringa hē, kāore rānei e noho wātea mō ake tonu.

Ko ngā Hononga Karere Huna he taputapu kaha mō te tangata e hiahia ana ki te tohatoha kōrero matatapu ki te haumaru. Mā te whakakotahi i ngā āhuatanga haumaru o te pae teitei ki te hoahoa hoa-kaiwhakamahi, ka whakarato rātou i tētahi rongoā kore taurite mō te whakawhitiwhiti matatapu i te ao matihiko.
