## He aha ngā Hononga Karere Huna?

Ko ngā Hononga Karere Huna he URL ahurei, whakawareware-ā-ia e taea ai e koe te tohatoha kōrero matatapu ki te haumaru. Ka taea te uru atu ki ia hononga kotahi anake i mua i te ngaro mō ake tonu, e whakaū ana kāore e noho tō raraunga kua tohatohahia i runga i te ipurangi.

## Pēhea te Mahi o ngā Hononga Karere Huna

1. Ka waihanga koe i tētahi karere huna i runga i tō mātou papatohu.
2. Ka whakaputa mātou i tētahi hononga ahurei, muhumuhu mō taua karere.
3. Ka tohatoha koe i tēnei hononga ki tō kaiwhiwhi i hiahiatia.
4. Ka tuwhera te kaiwhiwhi i te hononga ki te tiro i te karere.
5. I muri i te tirohanga, ka whakakorehia te hononga me ōna ihirangi mō ake tonu.

## Ngā Painga Matua

- **Whakamahinga Kotahi-Wā**: Ka mahi ia hononga kotahi anake, e ārai ana i te urunga kāore i whakaaetia.
- **Whakakorehanga Aunoa**: Ka mukua te ihirangi i muri i te tirohanga, kāore he tohutohu matihiko e toe ana.
- **Tohatoha Haumaru**: He pai mō ngā kupuhipa, ngā karere tūmataiti, me ētahi atu raraunga matatapu.
- **Māmā me te Hoa-Kaiwhakamahi**: Kāore e hiahiatia he pūkete mō te whakamahi taketake.

## Ngā Keehi Whakamahi

He pai ngā Hononga Karere Huna mō:

- Te tohatoha i ngā taunaha takiuru ki te haumaru
- Te tuku kōrero matatapu ki ngā hoa mahi
- Te whakawhiti raraunga whaiaro matatapu
