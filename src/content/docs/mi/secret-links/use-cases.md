title: Ngā Keehi Whakamahi
description: Tirotirohia ngā horopaki rerekē e taea ai e ngā Hononga Karere Huna te whakanui i te haumaru me te whakamāmā i te tohatoha kōrero matatapu.
---

He ngāwari ngā Hononga Karere Huna, ā, ka taea te whakamahi i te maha o ngā wāhi e nui ana te tohatoha haumaru, kotahi-wā o te kōrero. Anei ētahi keehi whakamahi noa:

### 1. Te Tohatoha Kupuhipa Taketake i te Mahi

I te wā e hiahia ana koe ki tētahi huarahi tere, haumaru ki te tohatoha kupuhipa, kī API rānei, ko ngā hononga karere huna he rongoā māmā. Ahakoa ko ngā kaiwhakahaere kupuhipa te kōwhiringa pai rawa mō te whakahaere taunaha roa me te tohatoha tīma, ko ngā hononga karere huna ka whakarato i tētahi whakakē māmā ka taea e te tangata katoa te whakamahi me te kore rorohiko motuhake, pūkete rānei. Ko te āhua whakawareware-ā-ia o ēnei hononga e whakaū ana kāore ngā taunaha matatapu e noho i roto i ngā pouakaīmēra īmēra, i ngā hītori kōrerorero rānei i te wāhi ka taea te kino.

**Painga**: Ko tēnei huarahi ka whakarato i tētahi huarahi tere, kore-whakatūnga ki te tohatoha taunaha ki te haumaru i te wā e hiahiatia ana e koe. Ka whakakorehia ngā tikanga mōrearea pēnei i te penapena kupuhipa i roto i ngā īmēra kuputuhi-noa, me te pupuri i te tukanga tohatoha māmā, kore-whakatete mō te katoa e whai ana.

### 2. Ngā Kupuhipa Netflix me te WiFi

I te wā e tohatoha ana i ngā taunaha ratonga whakaahua, i ngā kupuhipa WiFi kāinga rānei ki ngā whanaunga me ngā manuhiri kāinga, whakamahia ngā hononga karere huna ki te whakawhiti haumaru i te kōrero. Waihangahia noa te hononga, ā, tukua mā ō taupānga karere. I te wā kua tirohia e rātou te kupuhipa, ka kore te hononga, e pupuri ana i ō pūkete ki te haumaru ahakoa ka uru atu tetahi ki te hītori karere i muri mai.

**Painga**: Ko tēnei ka whakarato i tētahi huarahi whai hua ki te tohatoha urunga me te kore kino i te haumaru roa. Ahakoa ka ngaro tētahi waea, ka hē rānei te pūkete karere i ngā marama i muri mai, kāore te kōrero matatapu e whakaatu nā te mea kāore i penapena i te hītori karere.

### 3. Kōrero Utu

I te wā e hiahia ana koe ki te tohatoha taipitopito utu pēnei i ngā kōrero kāri nama, wāhitau pūkete matihiko rānei ki ngā kaiwhakarato ratonga, ngā whakapā pono rānei, whakamahia ngā hononga karere huna kaua ko te pato kōrero matatapu tōtika ki roto i ngā karere, īmēra rānei. Waihangahia he hononga haumaru e kapi ana i ō taipitopito utu, tohatohahia ki te kaiwhiwhi, ā, i te wā kua uru atu rātou ki te kōrero, ka tangohia aunoa - e whakaū ana kāore ō raraunga pūtea e noho whakaatu i roto i ngā miro whakawhitiwhiti.

**Painga**: Ko tēnei tikanga ka tiaki i tō haumaru pūtea mā te whakaū kāore ngā kōrero utu matatapu e kohikohi i roto i ngā miro īmēra, hītori kōrerorero, karere kuputuhi rānei. Ka hoatu ki a koe te mārie o te whakaaro e mōhio ana kāore ō taipitopito pūkete e taumahatanga mēnā ka hē tētahi o ēnei ara whakawhitiwhiti.

### 4. Ngā Kī Raihana Rorohiko

I te wā e tuari ana i ngā raihana rorohiko kua hokona ki ngā kaimahi, kiritaki rānei, whakamahia ngā hononga karere huna ki te tohatoha i ngā kī whakahohe. Ka whakaū tēnei kāore ngā kī raihana uara e whakamahi anō māharahara, e tohatoha rānei i waho atu i tō rātou kaiwhiwhi i hiahiatia, ā, ka āwhina ki te aukati i ngā kī mai i te penapena i roto i ngā pūnaha īmēra i te wāhi ka taea te urunga kāore i whakaaetia.

**Painga**: Ko tēnei tikanga ka pupuri i te whakahaere i te tuari rawa rorohiko me te whakamāmā i te uru atu o ngā kaiwhiwhi ki ō rātou kī raihana i te wā e hiahiatia ana mō te whakauru, te whakahōutanga rānei.

### 5. Ngā Waehere Whakaora Rua-Āhuatanga

I te wā e whakatū ana i te 2FA i runga i ngā pūkete nui, penapena haumaru i ō waehere putunga/whakaora mā te whakamahi i tētahi hononga karere huna. Mēnā me uru atu koe ki ēnei waehere i muri mai nā te taputapu kua ngaro, ka taea e koe te maia kāore rātou i te noho whakaatu i roto i tō īmēra, tuhipoka rānei. Waihangahia he hononga karere huna hou i te wā kua whakamahi koe i ngā waehere ki te pupuri i te haumaru.

**Painga**: Ka whakarato i tētahi whakakē haumaru ake ki te penapena waehere whakaora i roto i te kuputuhi-noa me te whakaū ka noho wātea i te wā e tino hiahiatia ana mō te whakaora pūkete.

### 6. Ngā Tūtohunga Uiui

I te wā e tuku ana i ngā wero waehere, aromatawai hangarau rānei ki ngā kaitono mahi, whakamahia ngā hononga karere huna ki te tohatoha i ngā whakahau. Ka whakaū tēnei ka whiwhi ia kaitono i tētahi kape hou o te tūtohunga, ā, kāore e taea te uru atu anō i muri i te tirohanga, e āwhina ana ki te pupuri i te wairuatanga o tō tukanga tono kaimahi.

**Painga**: Ka whakamāmā i te tuari tūtohunga me te āwhina ki te whakaū ka whakaoti ngā kaitono i ā rātou aromatawai motuhake, ā, kāore e taea te tohatoha i ngā whakahau tino ki ētahi atu.

## Whakarāpopototanga

Ko ēnei keehi whakamahi e whakaatu ana i te ngāwari me ngā painga haumaru o ngā Hononga Karere Huna puta noa i ngā ahumahi rerekē me ngā horopaki. Mā te whakamahi i ngā Hononga Karere Huna, ka taea e ngā whakahaere me ngā tangata takitahi te whakanui nui i ō rātou tikanga haumaru kōrero me te whakaitihia i ngā mōrearea e pā ana ki te tohatoha raraunga matatapu.

## Tuhipoka mō ngā Tohutohu Kore-Ingoa

Ka taea e ngā Hononga Karere Huna te tika mō ngā tohutohu kore-ingoa taketake me ētahi whakatūpato nui:

### Ngā Painga o Onetime Secret mō ngā Tohutohu Kore-Ingoa
- Kāore e hiahiatia he pūkete
- Ka taea te uru mā te whatunga Tor
- Ka muhumuhu te ihirangi i te kawe me te noho
- Te wā pupuri rārangi whāiti (tata ki te 2 wiki)
- Kāore e taea te tiki i te wā kua tirohia

### Ngā Whāititanga
- Whakaarohia ngā whakahau whakahaere/ture
- Kāore he ara whakawhitiwhiti kore-ingoa kua hangaia
- Ka taea te rārangi metadata taketake mō te wā poto
- Ko te kore-ingoa taumata-whatunga e whakawhirinaki ana ki te OPSEC kaiwhakamahi

## Ngā Kaiwhiuwhiu me ngā Horopaki Mōrearea Whaiaro

Mō te whiuwhiu e pā ana ki ngā take whakahaere, ture matatapu rānei, ka tino tūtohu i ngā papatohu whiuwhiu whakatuwhera me ngā tiaki tāpiri. I te wā e whakahaere ana i ngā whakaatu matatapu:

- Whakamahia ngā papatohu whiuwhiu whakatuwhera e whakarato ana i ngā tiaki haumaru whānui me ngā āhuatanga kore-ingoa
- Kia mōhio ko ngā wāhitau IP me ngā wā urunga ka taea te rārangi e ngā pūnaha rerekē
- Whakaarohia te whakawhiti whakaaro ki ngā whakahaere pono pēnei i te Freedom of the Press Foundation (freedom.press), te Electronic Frontier Foundation (eff.org) rānei mō ngā arataki taipitopito mō ngā tikanga whiuwhiu haumaru me ngā taputapu
