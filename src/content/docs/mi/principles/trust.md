# Te Pūataata me te Pono

E whakapono ana mātou ka whiwhi te pono mā te pūataata tino mō ā mātou tikanga, hangarau, me ngā here.

## Ō Mātou Here Pūataata

- Tuwhera-puna ō mātou hangarau matua
- Pānuitia ngā kaupapahere tūmataiti mārama, mārama
- Whakaratohia ngā pūrongo haumaru me te hanganga taipitopito
- Pupuritia he kōrerorero haere tonu ki tō mātou hapori kaiwhakamahi
- Kaua e whai i ngā tikanga aroturuki me te hokohoko paerewa
- Pupuritia ō raraunga i raro i te whakahaere tōtika

## He Aha Te Tikanga o te Pūataata

I tētahi wā o ngā ratonga matihiko uaua, ka tū ke mātou mā te tino mārama mō te āhua o tā mātou whakahaere raraunga, ngā mea e mahia ana e mātou, me te take e mahi ana mātou. Ko tō mātou huarahi e arotahi ana ki ngā mātāpono matua e toru:

1. Whakaute i te tūmataiti: Ko ō raraunga nōu, mutu kē
2. Arotahi ki te pono: Me mahi noa te taputapu i te wā e hiahiatia ana e koe
3. Hanga pono mā te mahi: Ka whakaatu mātou i tō mātou here mā ngā mea e karo ana mātou

## Tō Mātou Huarahi Matatapu-Tuatahi

Ka karo whakaaro nui mātou i ngā tikanga ahumahi noa e taea ai te kino i te tūmataiti:

- Ngā pūkete tātari e aroturuki ana i te whanonga kaiwhakamahi
- Ngā pātene whakaurunga pāpāho pāpori
- Ngā whatunga pānuitanga
- Ngā taputapu whakahaere aunoa hokohoko
- Ngā momo aroturuki tuatoru

## Te Titiro Whakamua

Ka haere tonu mātou i te whakarite i te pūataata me te tūmataiti i mua i te toro hokohoko. Ahakoa ko tēnei pea ka tupu māmā ake mātou i ngā ratonga e hokohoko kaha ana i a rātou, e whakapono ana mātou he kōwhiringa tika mō te hanga pono roa ki ō mātou kaiwhakamahi.
