# Ngā Mātāpono

Nau mai ki ngā mātāpono arataki a Onetime Secret. Ko ēnei tuhinga tūāpapa e whakamārama ana i tō mātou huarahi ki:

- Te tūmataiti me te whakaitihia raraunga
- Te whakawhitiwhiti kaiwhakamahi me te pūataata
- Te pono ratonga me te pono
- Ngā tikanga whakahaere

## Ō Mātou Here

- **[Matatapu Tuatahi](/mi/principles/privacy-first)**: Tō mātou huarahi ki te tiaki i te tūmataiti kaiwhakamahi mā ngā kōwhiringa hoahoa whakaaro nui
- **[Te Hanga Pono](/mi/principles/trust)**: Pēhea mātou e whiwhi ana, e pupuri ana hoki i te pono kaiwhakamahi mā ngā mahi, ehara i ngā kupu
- **[Te Kaupapa Whakawhitiwhiti](/mi/principles/communication)**: He aha mātou e whāititi ana i te whakawhitiwhiti īmēra ki te mea e hiahiatia ana anake
- **[Te Whakaitihia Raraunga](/mi/principles/data-minimization)**: Tō mātou here ki te kohikohi me te pupuri i ngā raraunga tino nui anake

## He Aha Te Tikanga o ngā Mātāpono

I Onetime Secret, ko ō mātou mātāpono e ārahi ana i ia whakatau e mahia e mātou. Ka āwhina rātou i a mātou ki:

- Te noho arotahi ki ngā mea nui
- Te whakatau pūmau
- Te hanga pono roa
- Te pupuri pūataata

Kāore ēnei he aratohu noa - he here ki ō mātou kaiwhakamahi e hanga ana i te āhua o tō mātou whakahaere.
