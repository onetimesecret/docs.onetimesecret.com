Nau mai ki Onetime Secret Docs, tō rauemi matua mō te whakanui i te uara o tā mātou ratonga tohatoha karere huna matatapu-tuatahi, poto.

## Ngā Mea Kei Konei

- Tohutohu: Ngā aratohu mārama, poto mō te whakamahi i Onetime Secret
- Pae ipurangi: Ngā mōhiotanga mō te tūmataiti, haumaru, me ngā whakahōutanga papatono
- Ngā Keehi Whakamahi: Ngā tono whaihua o Onetime Secret i roto i ngā horopaki rerekē (kei te haere mai)

## Ngā Mahi Matua

Ka whakarato a Onetime Secret i tētahi rongoā tōtika mō te tohatoha kōrero matatapu ki te haumaru:

1. Urunga kotahi-wā: Tirohia ngā karere huna kua tohatohahia, kotahi anake, kātahi ka mukua mō ake tonu
2. Kāore e hiahiatia te rēhita: Whakamahia ngā āhuatanga taketake me te kore pūkete
3. Paunga ritenga: Whakahaerea te wāteatanga karere huna ki te whakamutunga aunoa
4. Atanga māmā: Tohatohahia ngā karere huna māmā ki tā mātou hoahoa hoa-kaiwhakamahi
5. Kupu karapa kōwhiringa: Tāpirihia tētahi papa haumaru tāpiri ki ngā karere huna kua tohatohahia
6. Puna-tuwhera: Arotakehia tō mātou puna waehere mō te pūataata whakanui

Tirotirohia ō mātou Ihirangi Pōnaki hei ako i te āhua ka whakatau a Onetime Secret i ō hiahiatanga whakawhitiwhiti haumaru, whai hua, me te noho huna.

## Tīmata

Kei te rite ki te ruku? Tirohia ō mātou [tohutohu](/docs) mō ngā tohutohu taipitopito mō te whakamahi i Onetime Secret. Mēnā he pātai, he urupare rānei ōu, kaua e whakamā ki te [whakapā mai](/contact) tōtika.
